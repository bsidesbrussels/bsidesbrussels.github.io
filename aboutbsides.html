About Bsides Brussels

Security Conference in the heart of Belgium

BSides Brussels is a security conference in Belgium organized by BSides Brussels vzw., an official non-profit organization. The conference hosts talks and workshops in the field of security. The goal is to strengthen the exchange of knowledge, cooperation, communication, and integration between the different actors active in the IT security industry.

We are pleased to announce that the first edition of BSides Brussels will be held on May 28th, 2020
