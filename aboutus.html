About Us

The BSides Brussels crew consists of people working in the Belgian infosec industry, all sharing a common goal of creating an accessible, inclusive event focussed on learning and sharing around cybersecurity. We believe the BSides framework is a perfect fit since it focuses on serving and representing the local community while still being tied to the international network of diverse, independent security events that create opportunities to push the security field beyond its borders continuously.
