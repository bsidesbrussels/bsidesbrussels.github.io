Location

Odisee - Campus Brussels (Hogeschool-Universiteit Brussel)
Warmoesberg 26, 1000 Brussel



Directions can be found on the website of Odisee or below.

How to get there - by train

The train stations Brussels-North & Brussels-Central are at walking distance.

  • From Leuven it will take you 25 minutes to get to Brussels-Central.
  • From Antwerp & Ghent it will take you 35 minutes
  • From Bruges it will take you 1 hour
  • From Hasselt it will take you 1 hour 15 minutes

Just enough time to read your favorite book or newspaper.

Brussels-Central is right at the historical heart of Brussels (Grote markt, Manneken Pis) and a 5 minute walk from Campus Brussels. To get to the campus, follow the Keizerinnenlaan . This large street can be recognized by the many taxis who park right next to the station. Take the fourth street to the left, the Stormstraat. Before taking a left at this street, you can see the cathedrals of Saint-Michel & Saint-Goedele at your right. The buildings Hermes, T'Serclaes and Erasmus of the Brussels Campus are located at the intersection of the Stormstraat & the Warmoesberg.


How to get there - by foot or bike

The campus is situated at walking distance of the Brussels-Central train station. In front of the T'Serclaes building (Warmoesberg) there is a Villo-station. There's also a bicycle parking available in the T'Serclaes & Hermes buildings.


How to get there - by car

  • When on the small ring of Brussels - at Wetstraat, follow the direction "Centre".
  • Go straight ahead at the next traffic lights (Warandepark is at your left)
  • Take a right after the second trafic lights & take a 90 degree turn around the Saint-Goedele Cathedral
  • Take the first street on your left
  • At the traffic lights, go straight. You now enter the Stormstraat. The buildings Hermes, T'Serclaes and Erasmus of the Brussels campus are located at the intersection of Stormstraat & Warmoesberg.


How to get there - by metro

The metro lines 1 and 5 both have a stop called "Central Station". To get to the campus, follow the Keizerinnenlaan . This large street can be recognized by the many taxis who park right next to the station. Take the fourth street to the left, the Stormstraat. Before taking a left at this street, you can see the cathedrals of Saint-Michel & Saint-Goedele at your right. The buildings Hermes, T'Serclaes and Erasmus of the Brussels Campus are located at the intersection of the Stormstraat & the Warmoesberg.

More information about the metro can be found on the website of the MIVB.


How to get there - by bus

The lines 29, 38, 42, 63, 66 and 71 stop right in front of Campus Brussels in the Stormstraat (stop "Storm/Assaut"). You can find an overview of the lines on the website of the MIVB
