Format

We accept the following formats:

  • Lightning talks 5-15 min for various topics in security and privacy, but can even include an interesting home/research project.
  • Deep dive talks 25 or 50 min + 5 min Q&A
  • Workshops 1 to 3 hours

Process

The CFP is run blind, and no reviewer will know the presenter of a talk until after it is fully rated and accepted. As part of your talk description, please include a detailed outline.
